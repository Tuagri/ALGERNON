About

My name is Tuana, and I’m currently majoring in neuroscience. I’ve realized that I’m much more engaged with learning when I know I’ll be sharing what I learn with others. So, I created this website to help myself do what I like more effectively.

I am majoring in neuroscience because I am interested in intelligence and creativity. I think even the problems that seem impossible to resolve can be resolved with a little bit of spark—and that fascinates me. I don’t know what creates that spark or how it can be achieved, but I know, knowing more definitely helps it. So, here is a website that helps you and me start from somewhere.

The main goal with this project is to spread core scientific knowledge that everyone should know. I believe that even topics that seem unrelated to everyday life can offer new perspectives on the most ordinary things. Gaining general knowledge matters more than most people realize. So, I wanted this website to be a place where people can explore seemingly unrelated topics that spark curiosity and might just change how the world is seen.

Whether you're just curious or deeply passionate about science, I hope you find something here that you will remember later.

One last thing: no sentences on this website are AI generated. I only use AI to fix my grammar, not to create content. I think this will be obvious once you read the articles, they’re not exactly perfect.

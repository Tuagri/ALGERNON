About

My name is Tuana. I am currently majoring in neuroscience because I am interested in intelligence and creativity. I think even the problems that seem impossible to resolve can be resolved with a little bit of spark—and that fascinates me. I don’t know what creates that spark or how it can be achieved, but I know, knowing more definitely helps. So, here is a website that helps you and me start from somewhere.

The main goal with this project is to spread core scientific knowledge that everyone should know. I believe that even topics that seem unrelated to everyday life can offer new perspectives on the most ordinary things. So, I wanted this website to be a place where people can explore seemingly unrelated topics that spark curiosity and might just change how they see the world.

Whether you're just curious or deeply passionate about science, I hope you will find something here that you will remember later.

One last thing: no sentences on this website are AI generated. I only use AI to fix my grammar, not to create content. This will be obvious to you once you read an article, they’re definitely not perfect.
